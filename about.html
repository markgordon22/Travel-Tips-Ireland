About

Ireland has so much to offer!

You will not visit a country which has more of a perfect blend of beautiful scenery, an abundance of prehistoric mediavel sites, outdoor adventure activities and fabulous nightlife!

The Irish are friendly people!

One thing you will notice when you visit Ireland is how friendly the people are! The Irish are known around the world as one of the friendliest to get to know! When you arrive, you will be greeted with the warmest of welcomes!

It's not a massive strain on the budget!

Of course there are places in the city centres which are more expensive than others, however, if you plan your trip carefully, there are many gorgeous locations for you to visit without having to break the bank!
